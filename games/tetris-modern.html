🧩 How to Play

🕹️ Controls:

• Arrow Keys or WASD to move and rotate
• Spacebar for hard drop (instant drop)
• P to pause/resume, N for new game
• Swipe gestures on mobile devices

🎮 Gameplay:

• Arrange falling blocks to complete horizontal lines
• Completed lines disappear and give you points
• Game speeds up as you advance levels
• Don't let blocks reach the top!

🏆 Scoring:

• 1 line = 100 × level points
• 2 lines = 300 × level points
• 3 lines = 500 × level points
• 4 lines = 800 × level points
