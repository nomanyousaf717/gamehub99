🧩 How to Play Block Puzzle Classic

Objective: Fill complete rows or columns to clear them and score points!


Controls:

  • 🖱️ Drag pieces from the bottom area to the board
  • 📱 On mobile: Touch and drag to move pieces
  • 🔄 Pieces cannot be rotated - plan carefully!

Scoring:

  • 🧩 Points for placing each piece
  • 💥 Bonus points for clearing lines
  • 🔥 Combo multiplier for clearing multiple lines
  • ⭐ Level up bonus every 10 lines cleared

Strategy Tips:

  • 🎯 Try to clear multiple lines at once
  • 🤔 Plan ahead - no time pressure!
  • 🧠 Keep the board as clear as possible
  • 💡 Use the hint system if you get stuck
